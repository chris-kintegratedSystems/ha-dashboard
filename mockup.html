Design Decision Notes — For Chris's Review
🔑 Security Changes
Back door shown Unlocked (amber) — easy to spot vs. locked (cyan). Garage Left = Open to demo the red alert state. Confirm: do you want tap-to-lock with a confirmation dialog, or direct tap?
🌡 Climate Layout
4-column works on Tab S9+, stacks to 2×2 on iPhone. Current temp shown large (left) + setpoint with ± buttons (right). Confirm: want fan mode displayed? Want ability to switch heat/cool/off from the card?
💡 Light Groups
Grouped into Kitchen, Living Room, Outdoor, Bedrooms. Master toggle per room + individual items. Confirm: Are there existing HA group entities, or do we build light.kitchen_group etc. in configuration.yaml?
⚡ Quick Actions
5 scene shortcuts at top. Confirm which of these scripts/scenes exist in your HA already vs. need to be created. "Goodnight" would lock all + arm home + lights off. "Away Mode" arms away + locks + sets HVAC eco.
📷 Camera Views
3-column layout, simulated with SVG scenes. Real build uses camera_view: auto. Tap opens browser_mod popup with full stream. OK to keep same 3-col layout, or prefer 2+1 (doorbell bigger)?
💧 Rachio
Shown as a status card in the bottom row. Confirm: Is Rachio connected to your HA instance? If not, this card will be replaced with a "Benjamin's room" scene card or removed. Let me know.
